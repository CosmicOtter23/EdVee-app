Welcome to EdVee. Whether you are an experienced educator, curriculum developer, or new to curriculum design, EdVee has been created to make the process of designing modules and courses seamless and effective.

EdVee is offered here as an open-source application with a unique interface. One of the standout features is the ability to generate models which visualise the underlying pedagogical structure of a course. This feature allows designers to 'see' the pedagogical alignment between learning outcomes, assessment, content and learning activities.

The EdVee digital interface enables the designer to quickly review and edit a design to fully deliver on intended learning outcomes.

Digital EdVee designs can be duplicated to allow iteration, collaboration and evaluation of multiple ideas and then shared online for review.
